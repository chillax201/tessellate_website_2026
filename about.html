TESSELLATE
The Fest

Tessellate is Chennai Mathematical Institute's annual student-run fest where hundreds of attendees each year let loose and showcase their myriad of talents. We have amazingly geeky events as well as moments that remind us to touch grass. From Carnatic nights to trivia delights, tug of war to delicious food stall perusal, our fest tessellates cultural events and contests to create four days of unique experiences and incredible memories.
